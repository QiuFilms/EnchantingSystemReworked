## ✨ Enchanting System Rework
Enchanting System Rework overhauls how players obtain, apply, and find enchantments in Minecraft. The changes are made in vanilla-like manner to suit the gameplay seamlessly.
<br>

## 🌍 Dimension-Locked Magic
To encourage exploration, some enchantments are now tied to the dimensions.

Overworld: Standard enchantments found in everyday gameplay, with lower level.

The Nether & The End: Certain specific enchantments are now locked to these dimensions. You must travel to these dangerous lands to find the Scrolls or enchant your gear. The level of enchantments are higher in these dimensions.
<br>

## 📜 The Enchanted Scroll System
The core of this rework is the introduction of the Enchanted Scroll.

**Loot Rebalance:** Vanilla chests in Dungeons, Temples, and Cities now contain Enchanted Scrolls and lower chance of Enchanted Books.

**Villager Trading:** 
1. Librarians no longer sell Enchanted Books. Instead, they trade Enchanted Scrolls, which levels are bound to Overworld maximum enchantment level.
2. Armorers and Toolsmith sell enchanted gear, but with different chances to obtain muliple enchantents on item, and levels that are bound to Overworld maximum enchantment level.

![Enchanted Scroll as Trade Offer](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/ea471cd72ebe1e1bd203a078d2976448a4c78a36.png)

**Crafting:**  You can combine 3 same Enchanted Scrolls in a Crafting Table to craft Enchanted Book.

![Crafting Enchanted Books from Enchanted Scrolls](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/285af9f07200aa427f02d94e04408df2c45f2d12.png)
<br>

## 🔮 The New Enchanting Table
The Enchanting Table has been redesigned with a brand new Slot and mechanics.

The Scroll Slot: The table now features a dedicated slot for Enchanted Scrolls.

![Enchanting Table GUI](https://cdn.modrinth.com/data/cached_images/781d9d6ae86fdb26bb633c51f2cd83b236d0a888.png)

Guaranteed Enchantment: When you place an Enchanted Scroll into the table alongside your item and Lapis, the table guarantees 100% application of the Scroll's enchantment onto your item, in addition to the standard random enchantments generated by the table.

Available enchantments inside Enchanting Table are based on dimension, but some enchantments are not aquirable inside the table.

To use Enchanting Table it is required to use 15 bookshelves. Enchanting the item always costs 3 experience level and requres player to accumulate atleast 30 experience levels (Like 3 option in vanilla Enchanting Table).
<br>

## 🍀 Luck Actually Matters
The Luck attribute and status effect have been incorporated into enchantment logic.

Better Loot: Having the Luck effect increases the probability of finding high-tier Enchanted Scrolls in loot chests.

Better Tables: Luck directly influences the Enchanting Table. Higher Luck increases your chance  to aquire multiple and higher-level enchantments. Dimension playes role in enchanting - Nether and End give you additional points to you base luck when using Enchanting Table.
